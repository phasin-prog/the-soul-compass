# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Thai- and English-speaking readers who want to study the human psyche through depth psychology, psychoanalysis, philosophy, and typology. They arrive to read slowly, compare ideas, and build a more rigorous understanding than personality quizzes can provide.

## Product Purpose

The Soul's Compass is a bilingual long-form knowledge space for reading the psyche in depth. Success means readers can discover a useful line of inquiry, remain focused through substantial prose, and leave with sharper questions rather than a simplified label.

## Brand Personality

Contemplative, rigorous, intimate. The experience should feel like a quiet midnight reading room: serious without being academic theatre, warm without becoming sentimental, and distinctive without distracting from the text.

## Anti-references

- Personality-test funnels that reduce people to a type in a few minutes.
- Generic AI-generated editorial landing pages built from repeated cards, tiny uppercase labels, and ornamental serif styling.
- Neon wellness aesthetics, mystical gradients, glassmorphism, and decorative symbolism that imply certainty without substance.
- Dense academic portals that make serious material unnecessarily difficult to enter.

## Design Principles

- Let reading lead: typography, measure, rhythm, and navigation must protect sustained attention.
- Invite depth without intimidation: present rigorous ideas through clear language and progressive discovery.
- Make distinctions meaningful: color and hierarchy should clarify schools of thought, content types, and actions.
- Practice intellectual restraint: decoration must earn its place and never pretend to be insight.
- Treat Thai and English as equal first-class reading experiences.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA or better. Preserve browser zoom, keyboard navigation, visible focus, reduced-motion behavior, 44px touch targets, and sufficient contrast. Thai and English typography must remain legible at 200% zoom and across narrow mobile widths.
